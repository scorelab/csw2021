Hacking Challenge

The most awaited eCSW Hacking Challenge is back!

Enter the Hacking Challenge!

Instructions

The Hacking Challenge 2021 consists number of challenges in various cyber security domains. The participants are expected to penetrate into the given hacking environment and capture all the required flags. The team capturing the highest number of flags will be crowned as the winners of the Hacking Challenge 2021.

Date: 25 October, 2021
Time: 9.00 – 12.00
Venue: Virtual
Prices – Certificates, prices for winners & all participant will be given a certificate

Rules and Regulation

01. A team can consist of exactly 3 players.

02. Maximum three teams are allowed to participate from an organization.

Pre-requirements

01. All the participants are required to have their own devices.

02. Participants are responsible to have a stable internet connection.

03. All the participants are recommended to bring the necessary software tools (latest versions of the tools will be more effective).

04. Tools required for the hacking challenge may include but not limited to Windows environments, Linux environments, OS X environments, android environments, Digital Forensic tools and Penetration Testing tools. Example: Kali Linux, G track, Parrot etc.
