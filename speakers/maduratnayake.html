Madu Ratnayake

EVP, Chief Information Officer & General Manager, Virtusa

Madu is the Executive Vice President, Global Chief Information Officer and Center Head for Sri Lanka Advanced Technology Center at Virtusa. Madu is part of the executive leadership team that led Virtusa’s growth from start-up stage to $2B NASDAQ listed public company with over 28,000 team members globally and its recent successful sale to leading PE firm Baring Private Equity based in Hongkong. Madu leads all aspects of Virtusa’s digital strategy and focus on building Virtusa’s industry leading digital platform to accelerate client service delivery with growth over 20% CAGR over last 7 years. He is an accomplished

Madu represents the IT industry on the boards of several leading universities and serves as an advisor to number of industry chambers on export strategy. He is also a non-executive independent director of the Hatton National Bank, director of Sri Lanka CERT and director of Information Communication Technology Agency (ICTA), the National Agency for Digital Government.

Madu’s holds a Bachelor’s in Software Engineering (B.Eng. Honours) from City, University of London and MBA from Postgraduate Institute of Management, Sri Lanka. More recently he received an Executive Education in Digital Business Strategy from MIT Sloan School of Management.
